About Sanatan Seva Sangh

Sanatan Seva Sangh is a spiritual organization dedicated to the service of humanity based on the values of Sanatan Hindu Dharma. Our mission is to protect Dharma, serve society, uplift the needy, and spread the message of peace and compassion.

Our Seva Activities

Gau Seva

Daily fodder, medical care & protection for cows in our Gaushala.

Annadan

Food distribution to needy families, sadhus, and pilgrims.

Vriksharopan

Tree plantation drives for environmental protection.
