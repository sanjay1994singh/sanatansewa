About Sanatan Seva Sangh

Serving Humanity with Dharma, Seva & Sanskar

Who We Are

Sanatan Seva Sangh is a non-profit spiritual organization dedicated to promoting the eternal values of Sanatan Hindu Dharma. We work to preserve Indian culture, uplift society through seva, and spread the teachings of the Vedas, Puranas, and Hindu philosophy.

Our Mission

  • संरक्षण – Sanatan Dharma का संरक्षण व प्रसार
  • सेवा – गौ सेवा, अन्नदान, मानव सेवा
  • संस्कार – बाल संस्कार एवं नारी शक्ति कार्यक्रम
  • संस्कृति – भारतीय संस्कृति का पुनर्जागरण

Our Vision

“Vasudhaiva Kutumbakam – The whole world is one family.”

We envision a society guided by dharma, compassion, and unity.

Founder’s Message

"Sanatan Dharma is not just a religion, it is a way of life. Our purpose is to serve humanity with purity, devotion, and love."
